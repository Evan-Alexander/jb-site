About Me

I was a restless boy, always on my bike. Luckily I grew up in Portland, Oregon where such a thing is not only possible, but encouraged. I enjoyed the freedom my bike provided me. It was also invigorating. The speed, the wind on my face, the smells, the contstant change in scenery always moving slow enough to take it all in. It allowed me see things outside of the usual places my parents frequented. I became curious. I wanted to know what was around the next corner ... always. I've carried that sentiment with me to this day.
